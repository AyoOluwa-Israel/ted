About Event

What is TEDx?

TEDx is a global community of doers who believe in the power of sharing ideas. As a TEDx participant, you have full access to a globally networked platform--one that not only helps promote innovative thinking locally, but that spreads those ideas globally. We suspect you already know that participating in this event requires hard work and passion. Do you have the creativity and drive needed to bring a dynamic TEDx experience to your community. you’ve joined a vibrant community that’s ready and willing to support you every step of the way and push your ideas far and wide.



What edition of TEDxFUTA is the next one?

This is the third edition of TEDxFUTA event to be hosted in the FUTA community The second was themed "Lighthouse". While this edition goes with the theme "What Next?"



When will TEDxFUTA 3.0 hold?

TEDxFUTA 3.0 will hold on the 12th of February, 2022



Where will the event be coming up?

The event will be held at T. I. Francis Auditorium. The location was picked to conveniently contain the population of the audience expected to attend the event.



Will it be live-streamed?

Talks of Speakers will be live-streamed on our social handles to aid audiences outside the community get a feel of what's happening.



Is it only for FUTA students?

NO! The event is for everyone within and outside the FUTA community. The topics and Speakers were critically considered to cater for everyone.



What is the price of acquiring a ticket?

The ticketing price for the event goes for a token of #2,000. Included in the fee is access to the TEDxFUTA souvenirs, meal, and also the opportunity to participate in other activities at the venue.
